FAQ

About LIFT, specifically

How is LIFT different from The Scan?

The Scan diagnoses what's wrong. LIFT is the hands-on work that fixes it — embedded, ongoing, not a report you implement on your own.

Can I go straight to LIFT without The Scan?

No. The Scan is mandatory first — LIFT is scoped entirely around what it finds, so skipping it means guessing at the price and the plan.

How long does a LIFT engagement last?

It varies by what the business needs — typically measured in months. The exact scope and timeline are agreed after The Scan, in writing, before work begins.

Is Anthony personally involved?

Yes. LIFT engagements involve Anthony directly and embedded in the business, not delegated to someone you haven't met.

That's exactly what The Scan is for.
