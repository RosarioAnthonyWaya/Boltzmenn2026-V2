Entering the UK market without losing the culture that sells it

Client
Qate's Kitchen
Industry
Food & Beverage
Country
United Kingdom

Discovery

A strong African food identity, entering a UK market where the risk was real: lose the authenticity that made the brand worth knowing, or stay so tied to it that UK consumers couldn't connect.

Intervention

Brand positioning and visual strategy built specifically for cross-border relevance — drawing on lived experience across both Nigerian and UK contexts.

Impact

Positioned to grow in the UK market without diluting the identity that makes it worth choosing.
