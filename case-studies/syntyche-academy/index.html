Making the brand match the institution it already was

Client
Syntyche Language Academy
Industry
Education
Country
Nigeria

Discovery

Syntyche was already the premium language-education destination in its market — the teaching quality and reputation were earned. But the brand didn't reflect any of that.

Intervention

A full brand identity system was built — logomark, visual language, colour system, typography, and a communications architecture designed to scale as the academy grew.

Impact

Syntyche now presents as the institution it always was — consistent and professional at every touchpoint.
