Sharpening the pitch: how KET Shopping stopped losing the room

Client
KET Shopping
Industry
Personal Shopping & Procurement
Country
Nigeria

Discovery

KET Shopping was already a real, functioning business — Lagos personal shopping and procurement — but it had no visual identity. It was running faceless, with nothing distinct enough to make people remember it, pitch it, or trust it at first glance.

Intervention

We built the brand from the ground up and designed the website. Alongside that, we worked through sales and pricing strategy — so the business wasn't just easier to recognise, it was easier to buy from.

“We don't lose people halfway through the pitch anymore.”
Marcus Leigh
KET Shopping

Impact

A live, working brand and website at ketshopping.com, with a pricing structure that actually holds up in conversation.
