Holding operations together through a real growth phase

Client
Willy's Shawarma
Industry
Food & Hospitality
Country
Nigeria

Discovery

A tired founder was managing more than 50 staff members alone, while the business was actively expanding into new branches — with no operational backbone underneath any of it.

Intervention

For three months, Anthony stepped in daily as embedded Head of Operations — managing staff directly, resolving theft issues, and hiring for the new branches as the business grew.

“The clarity was worth more than the cost.”
Willy's Shawarma

Impact

Operational stability through a real growth phase, without the founder having to carry all of it alone.
