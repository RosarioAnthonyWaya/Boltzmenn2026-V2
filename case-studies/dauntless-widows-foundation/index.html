Giving a community its first home online

Client
Dauntless Widows Foundation
Industry
Nonprofit
Country
Nigeria

Discovery

Real, meaningful work supporting widows and their families — with zero digital presence. No way for anyone to find them, understand what they do, or feel part of it.

Intervention

A full site build, done alongside the founding team rather than handed off — designed to feel like the foundation itself: warm, purposeful, and rooted in community.

“Working with Rosario was quite amazing. He was very patient and walked with us each step of the way. We are more than happy with what our website looks like — a home online!”
Irene Ibiwari Ikiro
Founder & President, Dauntless Widows Foundation

Impact

The foundation's first full digital presence — a site that feels like the organisation, not a template applied to a cause.
