When the brand finally matched the product

Client
Chop Chop by Nanyaa
Industry
Food & Beverage
Country
Nigeria

Discovery

The product was genuinely excellent. The brand wasn't earning it — customers were buying on taste alone, with nothing in the visual identity that made them proud to share it or come back for more.

Intervention

A full brand rebuild: colour system, logomark, packaging redeveloped across every physical touchpoint, and brand guidelines delivered to keep it consistent going forward.

“There's nobody that looks at our products now that doesn't first compliment the branding and packaging. I've never been a fan of yellow but guess who is repping it with pride? ME! All thanks to Rosario for the excellent job.”
Nwaosa Joy
Founder, Chop Chop by Nanyaa

Impact

The brand became the product's biggest selling point. New customers began commenting on the packaging before tasting the food.
