Introduction
Boltzmenn values clarity, transparency, and responsibility — in our advisory work, and in how we handle your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what rights you have over it.
Information We Collect
  • Contact details you provide (name, email, phone, business name).
  • Business information shared during The Scan or any other diagnostic or advisory engagement.
  • Financial information shared as part of Financial Clarity or Circle engagements, where applicable.
  • Technical data (cookies, usage analytics) collected to improve this website.
How We Use Your Information
  • To respond to inquiries and deliver the services you've engaged us for.
  • To prepare and deliver Scan reports, strategy recommendations, and ongoing advisory work.
  • To improve our website, content, and how we communicate with founders.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements under Nigerian law.
Data Storage & Retention
  • Data is stored securely and access is limited to team members directly involved in your engagement.
  • We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law.
Sharing & Third Parties
  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Data may be shared with trusted operational providers (hosting, payment processing, analytics) only where necessary to deliver our services.
  • Where a Boltzmenn engagement involves a named partner (for example, an accountant on a Financial Clarity engagement), relevant information is shared only with your knowledge.
Your Rights
  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
  • Request details of how your data is processed.
