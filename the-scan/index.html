FAQ

What exactly do I get at the end?

A written diagnostic report covering all five dimensions we look at, plus a clear recommendation on what to do next — whether that's a specific service, or nothing at all if the business doesn't need one.

No. The Scan is a standalone engagement. You keep the report either way, and what happens next is entirely your call.

Pre-Scan homework is sent right after payment. The session itself is scheduled once that's complete, and the report follows shortly after.

Yes. Engagements are governed by written agreements, and your information is used only for the work we're doing together. See our Privacy Policy for details.
