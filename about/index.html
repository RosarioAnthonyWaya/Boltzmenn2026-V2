Business Rescue and Growth for African SMEs.

A Firm Built for Turning Around Businesses, Growth, and Expansion
Boltzmenn is a business rescue and growth firm working with African small and medium-sized enterprises that are under pressure, growing unevenly, or struggling with internal breakdowns.

We operate where decisions carry consequences — across finance, operations, execution, sales, and leadership. Our work begins when businesses are active, complex, and too important for guesswork.

We are not an agency.
We do not sell momentum.
We exist to restore clarity, stability, and durability inside businesses.

How we work

We don't advise from a distance. We work inside the business with you.

Every engagement starts with a structured diagnostic — not assumptions. We examine finance, operations, team performance, sales, and customer experience to find the real bottlenecks, not just the visible symptoms. Then we build alongside you to fix them systematically.
