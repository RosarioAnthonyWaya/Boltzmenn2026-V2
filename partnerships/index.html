Who We Are

Not an agency.
A growth institution in the making.

Boltzmenn is an Africa-focused business growth and business rescue company evolving into a structured SME support ecosystem. We help founder-led businesses move from confusion, weak systems, and founder-dependency into clarity, structure, and sustainable growth.

We began with roots in branding and business communication. But we quickly recognised that surface-level visibility was not enough for the businesses we cared about. Today, Boltzmenn brings together strategy, operational systems, customer experience development, sales support, financial clarity, and capital access — under one mission.

Our work is grounded in a simple but serious truth: most African SMEs don't fail because they lack ambition. They fail because they lack structure, systems, and the right support at the right time. We exist to close that gap — commercially, practically, and at scale.

Who We Serve

The founders at the centre of our work.

Our primary focus is African SMEs — specifically those already operating, generating some revenue, and facing real structural challenges that limit their growth. We serve four main founder profiles.

Profile 01

The Burnt-Out Founder

Businesses fully dependent on one person. No real delegation, no systems, no ability to scale without the founder present for everything.

Profile 02

Purpose-Driven, Revenue-Blind

Strong mission, genuine passion — but weak pricing logic, unclear revenue models, and financial decisions driven by emotion over commercial discipline.

Profile 03

The Expert-Turned-Founder

Highly skilled professionals excellent at their craft but struggling with the business side — systems, positioning, operations, and financial management.

Profile 04

The Growth Seeker

Businesses with existing traction that have plateaued. They need structure, clearer strategy, and repeatable systems to reach the next level.

A note for legal professionals.

Legal services sit at the heart of what African SMEs most commonly lack — and most urgently need. As a Boltzmenn delivery partner, a legal professional contributes meaningfully across several active areas of our work.

01

Business formation and structure — helping SME clients formalise, register, and structure their businesses correctly from the start.

02

Contract development and review — drafting supplier agreements, client contracts, employment terms, and partnership documents for our clients.

03

IP registration and protection — supporting founders with trademarks, brand registration, and intellectual property protection as their businesses formalise.

04

Funding and investment documentation — supporting businesses going through funding readiness, due diligence, and investor engagement via Boltzmenn Capital.

05

Business Clinic participation — contributing legal knowledge to our training programmes and founder education events as a subject-matter expert.
